Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

PhantomGrid is a memory game. We collect only what is necessary to run the game and leaderboard. We do not sell your data. We do not show ads (base version). We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifying information unless you voluntarily provide a display name.

1. Who We Are

PhantomGrid is an Android mobile game developed by an independent developer. The game tests your memory by flashing patterns on a grid which you must recreate.

For questions about this policy, contact us at: support@phantomgrid.app

2. Information We Collect

Anonymous Identifier

When you first launch PhantomGrid, we create an anonymous account using Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This generates a random unique ID (UID) that is not linked to your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information. This UID is used solely to:

  • Save your scores and progress to the cloud
  • Display your position on the global leaderboard
  • Link challenges between players
  • Maintain your Phantom Coin balance

Display Name (Optional)

After your first game, you may choose to set a display name that appears on the leaderboard. This is entirely optional. You may skip it or use any nickname. We do not verify display names and they are not linked to any real-world identity.

Game Data

  • Scores, Memory IQ ratings, and levels reached
  • Game mode (Classic, Time Attack, Zen)
  • Daily challenge completion status
  • Phantom Coin balance and transactions
  • Challenge history (who challenged whom, scores, outcomes)
  • Last active timestamp (used for "X players active today" counter)

Device Data

We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, advertising IDs, or location data.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location or GPS data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Device advertising ID (IDFA/GAID)
  • Browsing history
  • Financial information

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress and restore it across sessions
  • To display global and weekly leaderboards
  • To enable the challenge system between players
  • To calculate and display the "players active today" count
  • To calibrate the Memory IQ formula using aggregated (non-personal) score averages
  • To send local push notifications for challenges and reminders (on-device only, you can disable these in Settings)

5. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored in two places:

On your device: Game state, settings, coin history, and power-ups are stored locally using AsyncStorage. This data stays on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Firebase / Google Cloud: Scores, leaderboard entries, and challenge data are stored in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in Europe (eur3 region). Firebase is a Google product governed by Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Firestore security rules ensure that each user can only read and write their own data.

6. Push Notifications

PhantomGrid may send local push notifications for:

  • Daily play reminders
  • Streak protection alerts
  • Challenge received / accepted / completed alerts
  • Weekly performance summaries

All notifications are scheduled locally on your device or triggered by in-game events. We do not use third-party marketing notification services. You can disable all notifications at any time in your device Settings → Apps → PhantomGrid → Notifications.

7. Third-Party Services

PhantomGrid uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore database) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Expo / EAS (app build infrastructure) — expo.dev/privacy

We do not integrate with Facebook, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or any other third-party data brokers.

8. Children's Privacy

PhantomGrid does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation and collects no personal data by default. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the optional display name feature, contact us at support@phantomgrid.app and we will remove it promptly.

9. Data Retention

Anonymous game data (scores, leaderboard entries) is retained indefinitely to maintain the integrity of the global leaderboard. If you wish to have your data deleted, contact us and we will remove your anonymous UID and all associated records from Firestore within 30 days.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of all data associated with your anonymous UID
  • Request a copy of data associated with your anonymous UID
  • Opt out of the leaderboard by not setting a display name
  • Disable push notifications via device settings at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: support@phantomgrid.app

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of PhantomGrid after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
